Information We Access (But Don't Collect)

To provide our core functionality, the extension needs to access certain information locally on your device:

1. Current Website Domain

  • What: The hostname of the website you're visiting (e.g., "amazon.com")
  • Why: To check if deals are available for that store
  • How: Read via window.location.hostname
  • Stored: Never transmitted or stored anywhere

2. User Preferences (Stored Locally)

  • What: Your selected country preference (e.g., "US", "UK", "CA")
  • Where: Stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device only
  • Why: To show you relevant deals for your region
  • Shared: Never transmitted to our servers or third parties

3. Deal Data Cache (Stored Locally)

  • What: Cached deal information for websites you've visited
  • Where: Stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device only
  • Why: To improve performance and reduce server requests
  • Duration: Cached for 5 minutes for deals, 7 days for country list
  • Shared: Never transmitted to our servers or third parties

How the Extension Works

  1. You visit a shopping website (e.g., amazon.com)
  2. Our extension reads the domain name from the browser's address bar
  3. We query our deal database (via our background service worker) to check if deals exist
  4. If deals are available, we display a floating widget on the page
  5. When you click, we show you the available coupon codes and deals

Important: At no point do we read, access, or collect any information from the website content, your form inputs, or your personal data.

Affiliate Disclosure

This extension contains affiliate links. When you click on deal links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain and improve the extension.

Important: We never receive information about:

  • What you purchased
  • How much you spent
  • Your payment details
  • Your personal information

Permissions Explained

1. Storage

  • Purpose: Store your country preference and cache deal data locally
  • Data: Non-personal preferences and cached deals
  • Location: Only on your device

2. Active Tab

  • Purpose: Access the current tab when you click the toolbar icon
  • Scope: Only the currently active tab, only when you interact with the extension

3. Scripting

  • Purpose: Inject our content script as a fallback mechanism
  • Function: Displays our floating widget, never modifies website content

4. Host Permissions (All URLs)

  • Purpose: Automatically detect when you visit shopping websites
  • Access: Reads only the domain name (e.g., "amazon.com")
  • Does NOT Access: Personal data, form inputs, passwords, payment info
